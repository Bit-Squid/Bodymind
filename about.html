About Kimmi

Through intuition and empathy, Kimmi has helped many people in improving their mental, physical, and spiritual well being.

Kimmi has been building her intuitive abilities from a young age. She has always possessed talent for sensing the emotional energy of others. Her intuition would often guide her in life, influencing decisions, and illuminating the right path to follow.

Since 2002, Kimmi has supported the growth and well being of others through energy healing. She graduated from Ashmead Massage School in 2004. As a certified bodywork professional and an energy intuitive therapist she incorporates energy balancing along with somatic release, bodymind awareness, breathwork, massage and meditation. By combining these techniques, she takes you into your body so you can walk the path of self discovery. You can learn more about Kimmi's services here.
